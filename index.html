Impressum

This Website is my project for the austrian A-levels. The game is a tribute to H.P. Lovecraft's Cthulhu mythos and based on the rules for games like "Werewolf", "Mafia" and "Town of Salem". Developed with nodejs.

How to play:

One Player is randomly selected as Inspector, a few others as Spawns of Cthulhu and the rest are peasants. Every night the Inspector may investigate the role of one other player, after that the spawns decide which peasant to kill. Finally during the day, the peasants have to choose which fellow member of their town to accuse of murder and kill/send to prison (whatever floats your goat). After the last spawn has been dealt with, the peasants win the game and vice versa.
